Content that violates our policy includes:
Content that attacks, threatens, mocks, dehumanizes, or incites violence against members of the LGBTQ+ community.
Use of slurs, homophobic or transphobic language, or coded phrases that target LGBT+ people
Claims that LGBT+ identities are abnormal, sinful, or harmful
Misgendering or deadnaming intended to harass or belittle
Content encouraging conversion therapy or calling for exclusion, violence, or discrimination
Mocking or degrading representations of pride symbols, identities, or movements

We allow content that discusses LGBTQ+ in a positive or respectful manner
